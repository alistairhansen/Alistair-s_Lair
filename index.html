What's happening in the Lair

Hey, I'm Alistair!

Welcome to Alistair's Lair — a gaming community built around creativity, competition, and having a great time. I started this channel and server to share my love of building and gaming with people who feel the same way.

Whether you're here for the workshop builds, the build challenges, or just to hang out in the Discord — you're in the right place. Pull up a chair, grab some snacks, and make yourself at home.

What We're About

🏗️ Build Challenges

Every month we run a themed build challenge open to the whole community. Past themes have included medieval fortresses, sci-fi stations, and post-apocalyptic bunkers. Winners get featured on the channel!

🔧 Steam Workshop

I regularly upload builds to the Steam Workshop. Check the Workshop tab for current projects, their progress, and links to download them directly.

💬 Discord Community

The Discord is the heart of the Lair — community chat, build showcases, event announcements, and partner server links all live there. Join the link at the top of the page!

🤝 Partnerships

We partner with Discord servers that share our values — good vibes, creative communities, and a love of building. Interested in partnering? Drop a message in the Discord.
